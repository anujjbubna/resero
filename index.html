How it works

A practical path from student idea to market-ready concept.

Designed as a flexible foundation so future dynamic features like project listings, applications, and mentor matching can layer in naturally.

01

Spot meaningful problems

Students begin with what they are already studying, observing gaps, challenges, and opportunities worth solving.

02

Prototype in teams

Small teams validate ideas through fast experiments, feedback loops, and hands-on project development.

03

Access university support

Promising work is connected to the people, spaces, and programs that can help sharpen execution.

04

Move toward market

Teams prepare for pilots, partnerships, or venture next steps with a stronger foundation than a classroom project alone.
